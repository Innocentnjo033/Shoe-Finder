Tip 1: Proper Fit Matters

Always ensure your shoes fit comfortably without being too tight or loose. Your feet can swell during the day, so try shoes on in the afternoon for the best fit.

Tip 2: Rotate Your Shoes

Rotate between multiple pairs of shoes to allow them to dry out and maintain their shape. This also helps prevent excessive wear and tear on a single pair.

Tip 3: Choose Quality Over Quantity

Invest in well-made, quality shoes rather than buying multiple cheaper pairs. Quality shoes tend to be more comfortable, durable, and better for your feet in the long run.
